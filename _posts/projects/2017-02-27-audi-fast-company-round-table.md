Concept and installation design for a round table discussion on early adoption and autonomous driving hosted by Audi at the 2016 Fast Company Innovation Festival and located at Spring Studios. The event featured a custom-built round table, video graphics and presentations by Audi of America President Scott Keogh, New York City Chief Digital Officer Sree Sreenivasan, and design industry leader Doreen Lorenzo.
